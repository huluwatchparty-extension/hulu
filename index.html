Dive into Streaming: Your Guide to Starting a Hulu Party

This extension is meticulously crafted to ensure users enjoy a smooth and seamless streaming experience. Moreover, within just a few clicks, you can easily dive into the world of synchronized streaming. Whether you're new to Hulu Party or a seasoned streamer, this extension makes it simple to connect and enjoy your favorite shows and movies with friends and family, no matter where they are:

  • Install Hulu Party Firstly, you’ll need to install the Hulu Watch Party Chrome extension on your device to get started. This extension is compatible only with Chromebook, Windows, or macOS devices and works with Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browsers. Click here to install the extension. Don’t forget to pin Hulu Party to your toolbar for easy access.
  • Pin Hulu Party to Your Toolbar Once you’ve installed the extension, pin it to your toolbar for quick and easy access. Click on the “Extensions” icon, then pin the HWP button to your toolbar for seamless usage.
  • Log in to Hulu Afterwards, to start streaming together, sign in to your Hulu account. This will give you access to all Hulu content, including TV shows, series, movies, and more, so you can watch and enjoy with others online.
  • Find and Play Your Video Browse through Hulu’s extensive library to find the perfect video for your watch party. Once you've selected your desired show or movie, hit play to kick off the fun and enjoy a shared streaming experience with your friends and family.
  • Host a Watch Party Once your video plays, click the “Extensions” icon on your toolbar to open the “Create a Party” window. Next, select “Start the Party” to generate a party URL. Further, copy this URL and share it with your friends to invite them to join the fun.
  • Join a Hulu Party Log in to your Hulu account and click on the party link your friend shared with you. If you still need to install the Hulu Watch Party extension, you must do that next. Once installed, you'll be automatically redirected to the watch party, ready to join the fun.
